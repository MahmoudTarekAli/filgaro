About Us

Filgaro's roots go back to 1999, it was formed in 2013 in Istanbul. Our studio has over than 20 years’ of experience, and with the beginning of a new decade we are starting 2020 with a new brand full of strength, energy and creativity. We help transform brands and businesses through carefully crafted designs and rigorous strategic thinking.
